Westlund, a division of Emco Corporation, is Canada’s leading distributor of industrial pipe, valve, fitting and specialty products, servicing industrial contractors since 1963. We believe in “specialists serving specialists” as demonstrated by our dedicated team of solution providers with expertise in carbon and stainless steel, plastic and specialty alloys products.

We are proud to have the knowledge and experience to serve a diversified market including oil & gas, pulp & paper, mining, chemical, power, manufacturing, marine and water & waste management. We package, label, stage and deliver traceable quality products sourced directly from world class PVF manufacturing around the globe.

In January 2013, a joint venture was formed within Emco Corporation to offer contractors and end users the ability to sole source their materials for the water treatment and wastewater industry. Westlund/Emco Water Solutions distributes pipe, valves, and fittings (PVF) and other related equipment found “inside the fence” of the water and wastewater plants, reservoirs and pumping stations. The name “Water Solutions” really characterizes us as who we are, and what we do for our customers: we provide supply solutions in almost any material!
